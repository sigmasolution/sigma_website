Those people met up a few weeks later and Sigma was established. SIGMA has different names

People are definitely a company's greatest asset [...]. A company is only as good as the people it keeps ( Mary Kay Ash )

Senior Backend Developers in Python, C++, Java or Ruby

Job Description

What We Can Offer


- Competitive salary
- Annual Bonus
- Additional paid leave based on working time
- Training subsidy
- Good environment for developing technical and personal skills

What You Will Do


We are looking for ambitious, talented backend developers, who will join us to work on our many interesting and unique projects such as Social Networking Platform, Online Video Platform, P2P MarketPlaces or Internet Telephony for the our partners in EU and the USA. You will work in an international, young, creative, professional and friendly environment using cutting edge technologies.
Join us to benefit from:
- Unique, challenging projects where you can learn new technologies,
- Attractive and competitive salary with annual review,
- Annual bonuses, monthly allowances and Insurance based on the Labor Law,
- Company social and team building events,
- English courses subsidy,
- Flexible working time

Job Requirement

What You Are Good At Most of all, be a creative, open-minded person and a quick leaner with:

  • BS/MS degree in Computer Science, Engineering or a related subject,
  • 2+ years of experience in developing backend / RESTful services (either with Python, Ruby, Java, C++ or any equivalent programming language),
  • Sound object-oriented programming skills under Linux,
  • Good understanding of networking, multi-processing and micro service architecture,
  • Experience with large databases (using e.g. MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB),
  • It would be perfect if you are familiar with these technologies: Rabbit, Kafka, Celery, Docker, and Elastic Search,
  • Experience with Javascript, HTML and CSS is an advantage,
  • Good level of understanding in English (reading and writing technical specifications/documentations),
  • Distinctive achievements in Maths or Computer Sciences during your studies would be more than welcomed.
Preferred Language
(For Applications)		 English
